Securing Liberal Democracy

"We must defined democracy as that form of Government of society which is inspired above every other, with the feeling and consciousness as the dignity fo man". THOMAS MANN(1875-1955)

WHO WE ARE.

The Campaign for Women's vote in democracy is an vocacy and human development organisation (HDO) that is established mainly to empower and transform the status quo of women and yong people; provide psychological aid and counseling which has to do with the aspect of human thinking capacity in building and changing the narrative of sierra Leoneans, especially young peopel who are engage in illegal activities/drug abuse and violence in terms of the political dispensation in the country; as politics is a platform for choosing our representatives in a patriotic manner.

Moreover, Sierra Leoneans over the years and even in present day had been practicing a partisan politics which has affectd the securing of liberal democracy across the nation. Thus this organization is a non-partisan, non-sectarian organization that provide opportunities to improve the livelihood of individual women and families and thereby implement the ideology of patriotism and embrace the concept of nationalism ["nation's first"] as this will ensure the full development and advancement of women to exercise their individual or economic, cultural, civic or any other field. Since women are the most influential toolin nation's development; this organisation therefore seek to promote and improve the aspect of education which which has disturbed the participation of Women and young people in electioneering process.

MISSION

To inform people about democratic principles, good governance, civic rights and obligations; and to inclcate into minds the spirit of patriotism, and culture of tolerancee among supporters of diverse political party inclination and thereby encourage and enhance women and young people to pay and activeand responsible role in the electioneering process by raising pubic awareness through information and services that are reliable, applicable and sustainable

VISION

The people of a better future which we must all achieve is by enhancing liberal democracy; utilizing our resources and non-human resources and culture to develop economy. in other to achieve our goals; CaWVID strives to bethe best civic educcation promotion and conflict resolution services delivery agency in Sierra Leone and leading the way to national chesion; unity and election violence mitigate.

PHYLOSOPHY

The hall mark of this organization is the passion for patriotism. this passion create love for one another with loyalty to our country; that unite us together as one body rather than a service organization. Our faith is inspired by wisdom, our work is produce by love and our labour prompted by hope that Sierra Leon must be redeem and restore as the anthem of West Africa by Securing Liberal democracy for our children's future so that generation to come will continue in that trend. thus the Philosophy.

OUR OBJECTIVES

  • To raise high level of public awareness on democracy educationw ith special interest on frst time votes who have very limited pratical knowledge of or involvement in the electoral process

  • To equipped women and young people with the right information and sevices that will enable them to play an active and responsible role in choosing our representative in a patriotic manner in other to secure liberal democracy for better future and development of our nation

  • To encourage and promote the level of equility and create an enabling environment for the participation of all women in election process

  • To promote political tolerance among support (especially women) of divers political party inclination in the spirit of patriotism

  • To support and promote women's leadership and hereby inclucate into their minds the spirit of patriotism, and responsible citizenship across all themes.

  • To ensure the production and distribution of a wide range of civic education and voters registration materials in other to reduce the level of voter apathy
